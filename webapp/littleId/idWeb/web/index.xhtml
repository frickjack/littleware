Littleware Services

Welcome to services.frickjack.com - a set of online services that I hope to evolve over time.

littleId service

The littleId service acts as an openId assistant service that coordinates a web application's login process with an openId consumer hosted locally or at services.frickjack.com.

The littleId client (javascript, java, scala, and php) and openId are open-source under the LGPL license.
