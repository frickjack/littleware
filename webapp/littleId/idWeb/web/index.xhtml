Littleware Services

Welcome to services.frickjack.com - a set of online services that I hope to evolve over time.

littleId service

The littleId service acts as an openId assistant that coordinates a web application's login process with an openId consumer hosted locally or at services.frickjack.com to allow a client to authenticate with Google or Yahoo credentials.

Download the client SDK, and take a look at the demo to get started. The littleId code is open-source under the LGPL license.
