# TL;DR

Application patterns on the littleware architecture

## Asset management

Tracking versions, relationships, and workflows between different assets.  For example, publish a version of a document, kick of workflow to generate pdf, integrate into an approval and signing process, and associate multiple pdf's into a collection.

## Pamphlet

Storing different documents of a pamphlet where the content of different documents are managed by different application roles, but the pamphlet is read by clients as its own complete thing, and we have a library of pamphlets managing.

Ex:
* IoT - pamphlet document with stats from the device, and configuration from a manger
